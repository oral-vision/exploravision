# Our Solution: OralVision

<div class="box">
<p><strong>Utilizing deep learning to revolutionize oral diagnostics in underserved communities.</strong></p>
<p>OralVision leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to provide accurate, affordable, and accessible oral health diagnostics for communities that need them most.</p>
</div>

## Key Characteristics

OralVision offers superior diagnostic capabilities through innovative technology:

<ul class="alt">
	<li><strong>Machine Learning Algorithms</strong>: Identifies common symptoms of oral cancers and periodontal diseases with high accuracy</li>
	<li><strong>Non-Invasive Scanning</strong>: Scans the patient's oral cavity without discomfort</li>
	<li><strong>Specialized Cameras</strong>: Uses advanced cameras that account for distortion</li>
	<li><strong>Real-Time Analysis</strong>: Provides physicians with accurate potential diagnosis immediately</li>
</ul>
