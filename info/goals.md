# Goals & Strategy

<div class="box">
<p>Our strategic approach focuses on <strong>sustainable growth</strong>, <strong>widespread adoption</strong>, and <strong>positive impact</strong> in underserved communities worldwide.</p>
</div>

## Channels

We aim to attract a market for OralVision through strategic partnerships and distribution channels:

<ul class="alt">
	<li><strong>Healthcare Organizations</strong>: Reach out to established healthcare organizations like the Health Innovation Alliance for marketing support</li>
	<li><strong>Domestic Distribution</strong>: Use Amazon's FBA program for domestic distribution</li>
	<li><strong>International Logistics</strong>: Partner with Cencora WorldCourier for international logistics</li>
</ul>
